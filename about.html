Hobbies, Interests, and Things About Me!


Fishing


I've been an avid Salmon fisherman since I was a child.. my Grandpa has taught both my Dad and I how and where to catch Chinook Salmon while coming prepared with only a rod and basic tackle. We harvest all of our bait in the morning before we set out, and rarely ever come home without a Salmon to be smoked!


Track and Field


I ran sprints in track in High School and Collegiately. My events were the 100, 200, and 400 meter dash. The 400 was my best event, with a time of 48.68, but also my least favorite, which is a common theme for 400 runners.
