Hobbies, Interests, and Things About Me!



Fishing

I've been an avid Salmon fisherman since I was a kid.. my Grandpa has taught both my Dad and I how and where to catch Chinook Salmon while coming prepared with only a rod and basic tackle. We harvest all of our bait in the morning before we set out, and rarely ever come home without a Salmon to be smoked!

Track and Field

I ran sprints in track in High School and Collegiately. My individual events were the 100, 200, and 400 meter dash, but also ran the 4x100 and 4x400 relays! The 400 was my best event, with a time of 48.68.. But also my least favorite, which is a common theme for 400 runners.
